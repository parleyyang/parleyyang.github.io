My poster on the 20th May 2021, presented in the The Flip Side of the Pandemic: Recent Advances in the Mathematics of Information :
Adaptive Learning on Time Series: Theory and Practice
Abstract:
Modern data science deals with large dimensional modelling and prediction. On top of those, time series has its unique properties --- time-varying relationships and autocorrelation --- due to the nature of time. Working from a forecasting-centric standpoint, we propose a novel learning regime on time series, adaptive learning. In this poster, we collect and present the contemporaries from the works we have done [7,9,10] and to be done [11].
